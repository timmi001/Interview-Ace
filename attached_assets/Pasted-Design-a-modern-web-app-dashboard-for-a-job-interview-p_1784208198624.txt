Design a modern web app dashboard for a job interview preparation platform.**

Create a clean, premium, minimalist UI with soft shadows, rounded corners (20–24px), subtle gradients, and plenty of white space. The overall style should feel similar to Duolingo, Google Material 3, and Linear.

### Theme

* Primary color: #0357EE blue ribbon
* Background: White (#FFFFFF)
* Secondary colors:

  * Blue (#4F7DFF)
  * Green (#4CAF50)
  * Orange (#FF9F43)
  * Light Purple (#F5F3FF)
* Soft gradient backgrounds
* Glassmorphism-inspired cards with very light shadows
* Friendly illustrations
* Modern rounded icons
